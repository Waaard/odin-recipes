Sinigang

Description

This recipe is an easy to do Sinigang recipe you can make at home!!

Ingridients

  • Pork butt
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Lemongrass
  • Water
  • Jalepeño Pepper
  • Baby bok choy
  • Daikon radish
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Fish sauce
  • Kosher salt

Instructions

  1. In a 6-quart dutch oven, add pork and enough water to cover the meat.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over medium high heat. Boil for 2 minutes, then carefully drain pork pieces into a colander. Rinse out the pot, making sure to rinse out the brown residue from the pork. Rinse the pork with cold water then return to the cleaned pot.
  3. Add onions, garlic, tomatoes, lemongrass, and 9 cups of water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer. Cover the pot with a lid, leaving a little crack open. Cook for 60-80 minutes or meat is tender.
  4. Then add fish sauce, 1½ teaspoons kosher salt (or to taste), jalapeño, and daikon radish and cook for 20 minutes or until radish are fork tender.
  5. Stir in lemon juice, bok choy ends and cook for 2 minutes. Then add bok choy leaves and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste with more fish sauce, kosher salt and/or lemon juice, if necessary. Then ladle into bowls and serve with jasmine rice on the side. Enjoy!
  7. If desired, add 1 teaspoon of fish sauce and 1¼ teaspoons lemon juice to a ramekin and lightly spoon over the sinigang between bites.
